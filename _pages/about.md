Biography
======

Currently I am a M.Sc. student at the [School of Artificial Intelligence](https://ai.nju.edu.cn/), [Nanjing University](http://www.nju.edu.cn/), and a member of the [LAMDA Group](http://www.lamda.nju.edu.cn/CH.MainPage.ashx). The group is led by Prof. [Zhi-Hua Zhou](http://cs.nju.edu.cn/zhouzh/).

I received my B.Sc. degree in Brain Science and Artificial Intelligence from the [Kuang Yaming Honors School](http://dii.nju.edu.cn/), [Nanjing University](http://www.nju.edu.cn/), in June 2025. In September 2025, I was admitted to pursue the M.Sc. degree at Nanjing University without entrance examination.

Research Interests
======

My research interests include reinforcement learning, especially: 
- Continual & Multi-task reinforcement learning
- Multi-agent reinforcement learning.
